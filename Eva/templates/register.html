Our Mission

We all know that one of the most stressful things for students is deciding where to live in the coming school year. With multiple factors such as price, amenities, and location, trying to accomodate for everyone living together can be very tough. That's why Illini Housing Finder wants to make the process easier. By allowing students to create accounts with their preferences and create groups with other students, our algorithms take the information that each student enters to find the optimal apartment or university housing option that best suits everyone's needs.
